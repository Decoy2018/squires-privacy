Privacy Policy  |  Effective Date: March 1, 2026  |  Version 1.0

Summary. This Privacy Policy describes how Squire collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal information when you use the App. We collect information you provide (name, email, photos, location) and information generated by your use of the App (device tokens, usage data). We use your information to operate the platform, process payments, and communicate with you. We do not sell your personal information. We rely on Stripe, Firebase, Cloudinary, and Render to deliver the Services, and each has its own privacy practices. You have rights over your data and may contact us at any time.

1. Introduction and Scope

Squire ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices available to you in connection with the Squire mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Services, including Hosts and Guests. It does not apply to the practices of third-party service providers whose services we use, including Stripe, Google Firebase, Cloudinary, and Render. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those providers independently.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect the following categories of personal information that you provide when registering for or using the Services:

  • Identity Information: Full legal name and profile photograph.
  • Contact Information: Email address.
  • Location Information (Hosts): Physical address or pinned geographic location of your storage space (latitude and longitude coordinates stored in association with your Listing).
  • Listing Information (Hosts): Storage capacity (number of boxes), pricing per period, availability start and end dates, and a description of the storage space.
  • Vehicle Information (Hosts offering vehicle-related services): Vehicle make, model, color, and license plate number.
  • Financial Information: Bank account, debit card, or other payout information submitted directly to Stripe during Stripe Connect onboarding. Squire does not directly collect, store, or process credit card numbers or banking credentials.
  • Communications: Messages sent through the App's in-app messaging features.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Services, we and our service providers automatically collect:

  • Precise Geolocation Data: With your permission, we collect real-time GPS latitude and longitude data from your mobile device. This data is used to display nearby Listings and to allow Hosts to set their Listing location. You will be prompted to grant location permissions, and you may revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.
  • Device Push Notification Token: A unique token issued by your device's operating system used to deliver push notifications regarding bookings, messages, and platform updates.
  • Account Metadata: Timestamps for account creation, profile updates, and email verification events.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including features accessed and session duration, used to maintain and improve the Services.

2.3 Information Received from Third Parties

  • From Stripe: Stripe Connect account ID (stripeConnectAccountId), and payout-readiness status flags including chargesEnabled, payoutsEnabled, detailsSubmitted, and onboardingCompleted. Squire does not receive your full financial account details from Stripe.
  • From Firebase Authentication: Email verification status (emailVerified) and authentication event timestamps.
  • From Cloudinary: Public image delivery URLs (imageUrl) for profile photographs and Listing images stored on Cloudinary's infrastructure.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create, authenticate, maintain, and support your Squire account.
  • Platform Operations: To facilitate Bookings, display Listings, connect Hosts and Guests, and provide App features.
  • Payment Processing: To initiate and complete transactions through Stripe and to administer Host payouts.
  • Location-Based Services: To display nearby storage Listings and to allow Hosts to associate their Listing with a precise geographic location.
  • Push Notifications: To send transactional notifications, including booking confirmations, new messages, and account alerts. You may opt out of non-essential notifications in your device settings.
  • Identity and Safety: To verify users, investigate suspected violations of these Terms, and protect the safety and integrity of the platform.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal process, and governmental requests.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the functionality, reliability, and user experience of the App.
  • Communications: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and send service-related announcements.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

  • With Other Users: Host profiles (including full name, profile photograph, storage location area, Listing details, and host status) are visible to Guests browsing the App. Upon confirmation of a Booking, limited profile information of a Guest may be visible to the relevant Host.
  • With Stripe: We transmit to Stripe such information as is necessary to process payments, initiate payouts, and satisfy Stripe's identity verification requirements. Your financial information is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • With Google (Firebase): Your account data, Listings, Booking records, and messages are stored in Firebase Firestore. Authentication is processed through Firebase Authentication.
  • With Cloudinary: Images uploaded to the App are stored on and served through Cloudinary's infrastructure.
  • With Render: Our backend application server, hosted by Render, processes API requests made through the App.
  • As Required by Law: We may disclose your information where required by applicable law, court order, subpoena, or other legal process.
  • In a Business Transaction: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of Squire's assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information for any other purpose with your prior explicit consent.

5. Location Data

The App requests permission to access your device's precise GPS location (latitude and longitude). We collect this information:

  • When you, as a Guest, search for nearby storage Listings, to identify and display relevant results;
  • When you, as a Host, create or update a Listing, to associate your storage space with a geographic coordinate stored in your user record in Firebase; and
  • As necessary to provide and improve location-based features of the App.

Real-time location data used for search purposes is not retained beyond the immediate session. You may revoke location permissions at any time in your device's system settings.

6. Photographs and Image Data

Profile photographs and Listing images that you upload through the App are transmitted to and stored on Cloudinary's cloud infrastructure. The public delivery URL for each image is stored in your Firebase user record and may be displayed to other users in connection with your profile or Listing.

You should not upload photographs containing sensitive personal or financial information, identifying documents, or any images to which you do not hold the right to use and share.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active and for such additional period as is reasonably necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or de-identify your personal information within a commercially reasonable period. Aggregate or anonymized data may be retained indefinitely.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase security rules, access controls on backend systems, and restricted access to production databases.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

9. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to individuals under the age of eighteen (18). If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor, we will take prompt steps to delete such information. If you believe we may have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: The right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: The right to request deletion of your account and associated personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Data Portability: The right to receive your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out of Notifications: You may disable push notifications at any time through your device's system settings.
  • Location Revocation: You may revoke the App's access to your location data at any time through your device's system settings.

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us through the in-app support feature. We will respond to verifiable requests within thirty (30) days.
